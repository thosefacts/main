Bill & Melinda gates foundation fight Corona-virus

27 April 2020

Bill Gates announces a shift in his foundation's focus

   Bill Gates announced that his foundation will put all its efforts towards combatting the Corona virus. He mentioned the development of proper vaccines as the most important measure towards relieving the world of the corona-related problems.

   The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation has a fund of roughly 40 Billion dollars and was initally erected to alleviate problems caused by sicknesses such as HIV, Polio and Malaria.

Reaction to US-President

   Bill gates furthermore reacted to the statements of US-president Trump regarding the WHO. Gates claimed that the WHO had not mishandled the pandemic and should get all the support it needs to fight the ongoing health crisis. Perhaps he implied that the USA should not have cut its funding to the WHO.
