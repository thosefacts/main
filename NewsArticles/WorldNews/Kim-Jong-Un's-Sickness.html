Rumors about Kim Jong-Un's health: An Overview

27 April 2020

A summary of events regarding concerns about Kim Jong-Un's health

   Rumors started arising about Kim Jong-Un’s health when he was missing at ‘The Day of the Sun’ on 15 April. His absence raised concerns as the public holiday commemorates the birth of Jong-Un's grandfather, Kim Il-Sung.

   Over the past 2 weeks, there have been concerns about Jong-Un’s health across the globe. But, his actual state remains unclear.

A summary of events

China sent medical experts

   China sent a team of medical experts, to help with any health problems Jong-Un might be experiencing. This happened after rumors arose that Jong-Un may be experiencing cardiovascular issues.

Luxury train sighted at resort

   Last week, a satellite sighted a luxury train that Jong-Un uses for trips. The train was stationed in Wonsan at a luxury resort designated for the state leadership.

Reports about serious health problems

   A TV mogul of Hong Kong Satellite Television claimed that Kim Jong Un is dead.
Japanese media suggested that Jong-Un was in a vegetative state.

A Letter

   While Jong-Un has still not been sighted in public, he sent a letter in response to the concerns about his health. The letter expressed his satisfaction with the hard work of construction workers, developing the city of Samjiyon.

Moon Chung-in's statement

   A rather well-known Special Advisor to the South Korean president stated that Kim Jong-Un was alive and indeed resided at his resort in Wonsan.
